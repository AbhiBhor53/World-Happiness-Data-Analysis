Reccomendations

World Happiness Data Analysis clearly demonstrate following points:

Countries with good per capita income, healthy life expectancy and Social support have been consistently doing well in this list for 5 subsequent years. citizens of the country off worries about attaining basic living standards, healthcare are more likely to be genorous and help others wholeheartedly citizens who have been given old age allowances, unemployement allowances, disability support and other social support benefits to underpreviledged class more likely trust government and because of this social support schemes people are more free to make life choices. e.g Denmark with happiness Score=7.5460 has also performed in per capita income=1.383(aprx),healthy life expectancy=0.996(Aprx) and Social support=1.573(Aprx)

For countries to perform well in this index must work on uplifting GPD per capita income, Healthacare facilities and enhancing social support for weaker section of the society.

GDP per capita income, Healthacare facilities, Social support and trust on govenrment are 4 metrics largely responsible for position in the list all of these metrics are equally important. because there are some coutries who have performed exceedingly well in one of these fronts but failed to secure rank in high happiness level cluster. e.g. China, India depite being into club of 10 largest economies in the world couldnt secure high level happiness cluster.
